How It Works

📝

1. Story Engine

An LLM crafts a four-beat narrative — hook, build, climax & CTA — tailored to your genre and tone.

🎥

2. Scene Engine

The narrative is translated into a timed shot list with visual prompts ready for text-to-video models.

🗣️

3. Voice Engine

Each narrative beat is voiced using an AI TTS service and exported as a per-beat audio file.

🎵

4. Music Engine

Background music is selected or generated to match your genre — BPM, mood and mix levels included.

✂️

5. Editor Engine

Everything is assembled into an Edit Decision List (EDL) and an FFmpeg command ready to render.
